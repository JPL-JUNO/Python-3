'a(ab)' (a followed by literal ab)

  'abbaaabbbbaaaaa'
  ....'aab'

'a(a*b*)' (a followed by 0-n a and 0-n b)

  'abbaaabbbbaaaaa'
  'abb'
  ...'aaabbbb'
  ..........'aaaaa'

'a(ab)*' (a followed by 0-n ab)

  'abbaaabbbbaaaaa'
  'a'
  ...'a'
  ....'aab'
  ..........'a'
  ...........'a'
  ............'a'
  .............'a'
  ..............'a'

'a(ab)+' (a followed by 1-n ab)

  'abbaaabbbbaaaaa'
  ....'aab'
